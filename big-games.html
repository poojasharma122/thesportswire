NBA Events Calendar – Key Dates for the 2025–26 Season

Stay ahead of the biggest moments in basketball with our comprehensive NBA events calendar. From opening night to the NBA Draft, this page is your one-stop guide to every major date on the league calendar.

NBA Season Tip-Off

Date: October 21, 2025

The season begins with a nationally televised doubleheader featuring last year’s conference champions. Expect pre-game ceremonies, star-studded halftime shows, and early-season storylines that set the tone for the next eight months.

NBA In-Season Tournament

Dates: November 2025 – December 2025

The second edition of the NBA’s In-Season Tournament features group play, knockout rounds, and a championship game in Las Vegas. This format has quickly become a highlight of the early season, creating playoff-like intensity before the calendar hits January.

NBA Christmas Day Games

strong December 25, 2025

The league’s most anticipated regular-season slate. Five nationally televised matchups showcase the game’s biggest stars, often including Finals rematches and marquee rivalries. For many fans, the Christmas Day games are as much a tradition as the holiday meal.

Martin Luther King Jr. Day Games

Date: January 19, 2026

An important day on the NBA calendar that features a full slate of nationally televised games and tributes celebrating Dr. King’s legacy. Always a day filled with competitive matchups and special programming.

NBA Trade Deadline

Date: February 20, 2026

The last day for teams to make trades during the season. Rumors and last-minute deals dominate headlines as contenders look to upgrade for a title run and rebuilding teams stockpile draft picks.

NBA All-Star Weekend

Dates: February 13–15, 2026

Three days of basketball’s biggest celebration, including:

  • Celebrity Game & Rising Stars Challenge (Friday)
  • Skills Challenge, Three-Point Contest, Slam Dunk Contest (Saturday)
  • NBA All-Star Game (Sunday), featuring the league’s brightest stars in a high-scoring showcase

NBA Play-In Tournament

Dates: April 14–17, 2026

The 7–10 seeds in each conference fight for the final two playoff spots in a high-drama, win-or-go-home format. The Play-In has become one of the league’s most exciting innovations, keeping more teams competitive until the very end.

NBA Playoffs

Start Date: April 18, 2026

Sixteen teams begin the race to the Finals. From first-round upsets to seven-game classics, this two-month stretch is the pinnacle of basketball competition.
