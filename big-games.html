NBA Events Calendar – Key Dates for the 2025–26 Season

Stay ahead of the biggest moments in basketball with our comprehensive NBA events calendar. From opening night to the NBA Draft, this page is your one-stop guide to every major date on the league calendar.

NBA Season Tip-Off

Date: October 21, 2025

The season begins with a nationally televised doubleheader featuring last year’s conference champions. Expect pre-game ceremonies, star-studded halftime shows, and early-season storylines that set the tone for the next eight months.

Caesars Palace

★★★★★ | Classic Vegas casino hotel, top dining, entertainment, central location | Highly rated for nightlife

Caesars Palace combines Roman grandeur with modern amenities. The sprawling casino hotel buzzes with activity, complemented by a variety of bars, nightclubs, and high-end restaurants. Its central Strip location puts you close to many key attractions. A great choice for guests wanting lively nightlife and luxury all in one place.

The Venetian

★★★★★ | Spacious suites, gondola rides, upscale shopping, on the Strip | Breakfast available

The Venetian brings a slice of Venice to Las Vegas. Suites are large and elegantly decorated, offering plenty of space for relaxation. Don’t miss the romantic gondola rides through the resort’s canals or the upscale shopping area. This upscale casino resort is perfect for those who want a blend of charm and comfort.

Mandalay Bay

★★★★ | Massive pool complex with wave pool, family-friendly | Beach-themed casino resort

Mandalay Bay is a favourite for families and pool lovers. Its extensive pool complex features a wave pool and lazy river, providing a beach-like atmosphere right in the desert. The resort’s large casino hosts concerts and events, making it lively without overwhelming. Ideal for those who want fun and relaxation.

Wynn Las Vegas

★★★★★ | Elegant design, fine dining, nightlife | Award-winning service

Wynn Las Vegas is known for its sleek design and attention to detail. The rooms are luxurious and well-appointed, while the dining options range from casual to Michelin-starred. The nightlife is refined and engaging. This luxurious casino resort is perfect for travellers who appreciate style and top-tier service.

Aria Resort & Casino

★★★★★ | Modern rooms, serene pool area, tech-savvy amenities | Great for business and leisure

Aria blends contemporary design with comfort. Rooms feature smart technology, while the pool areas offer a tranquil retreat. The large casino is a key feature, with diverse gaming and dining options. This casino resort suits both business travellers and vacationers looking for a modern Vegas experience.

The Cosmopolitan

★★★★ | Trendy rooftop pools, chic rooms, lively bar scene | Popular with young travellers

The Cosmopolitan appeals to a younger crowd with its trendy atmosphere. Rooftop pools provide stunning views of the Strip, and the bar scene is buzzing every night. Rooms are stylish and modern, making it a hotspot for social travellers and nightlife seekers. This casino hotel blends entertainment with chic design.

Red Rock Casino Resort & Spa

★★★★ | Off-Strip, mountain views, spa services, quiet atmosphere | Good for families and relaxation

Set away from the Strip’s hustle, Red Rock offers a peaceful escape with scenic mountain views. The resort includes a full-service spa and golf course, making it ideal for those seeking relaxation. Family-friendly and less crowded, this casino resort provides a different pace without sacrificing quality.
