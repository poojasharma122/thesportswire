Personal Data Policy

At The Sports Wire (“we,” “our,” or “us”), we are committed to protecting your personal data and using it responsibly. This Personal Data Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, how we process it, and your rights regarding that data.

What Personal Data We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to operate the-sports-wire.com (“the Site”) and provide you with relevant basketball news, analysis, and updates. This may include:

  • Contact Information: Name and email address if you subscribe to our newsletter, submit a contact form, or email us directly.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device type, operating system, and pages visited.
  • Preferences: Newsletter opt-in status, cookie preferences, and region settings for localized content.

We do not knowingly collect sensitive personal data such as government ID numbers, financial account details, or health information.

Why We Collect Personal Data

We collect and process data to:

  • Deliver NBA news, fantasy basketball content, and event updates.
  • Send newsletters and marketing communications you opt into.
  • Monitor and improve site performance, usability, and security.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms & Conditions.

How We Process and Store Your Data

  • Legal Basis for Processing: We process personal data based on user consent (newsletter signup, cookies) and legitimate interest (analytics, security).
  • Data Storage: Information is stored securely using industry-standard encryption and access controls.
  • Retention Period: We retain personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes stated above or as required by law.

Sharing Your Data

We do not sell or rent personal data. We may share information with:

  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who assist with website hosting, analytics, and email distribution. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data.
  • Legal Authorities: When required to comply with legal obligations, court orders, or to protect the rights and safety of users.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (GDPR, CCPA, etc.), you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your data (“right to be forgotten”).
  • Restrict or object to certain data processing activities.
  • Withdraw consent for communications or cookies at any time.

To exercise these rights, email us at contact@the-sports-wire.com.

International Data Transfers

If you are accessing the Site from outside the United States, your data may be processed in countries with privacy laws different from your own. By using the Site, you consent to these transfers.

Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. While we work hard to safeguard data, no online system can guarantee absolute security.

Children’s Data

The Sports Wire does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a minor, contact us immediately and we will delete it.

Updates to This Policy

We may revise this Personal Data Policy periodically to reflect changes in regulations or business practices. The most current version will always be posted on this page with the effective date clearly marked.
