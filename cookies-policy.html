Cookies Policy

This Cookies Policy explains how The Sports Wire (“we,” “our,” “us”) uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit the-sports-wire.com (“the Site”). By continuing to use the Site, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help improve user experience by remembering preferences, personalizing content, and tracking site performance.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies
    These cookies are necessary for the basic functioning of the Site, such as enabling page navigation, maintaining security, and loading core features. Disabling these cookies may make parts of the Site unusable.
  • Performance and Analytics Cookies
    We use these cookies to collect anonymous data on how visitors use the Site — for example, which pages are visited most frequently, how users navigate between pages, and how long they stay. This helps us improve usability and optimize our basketball coverage.
  • Functional Cookies
    These remember your preferences (such as region or newsletter opt-in status) so we can personalize your experience.
  • Advertising and Affiliate Cookies
    Certain cookies track engagement with affiliate links or sponsored content. This allows us to measure conversions and provide relevant recommendations. Clicking these links may result in a commission for The Sports Wire at no extra cost to you.

Third-Party Cookies

The Sports Wire uses trusted third-party services such as Google Analytics to help analyze traffic and improve the Site. These providers may set their own cookies according to their privacy policies.

Managing Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Options may include:

  • Blocking all cookies.
  • Accepting only certain types of cookies.
  • Deleting existing cookies from your device.

Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some features, such as saved preferences or personalized recommendations.

Legal Compliance

This policy is designed to comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy regulations. Visitors in regions requiring consent will see a cookie banner on first visit with options to accept, decline, or customize preferences.
