Privacy Policy

The Sports Wire (“we,” “our,” “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you visit the-sports-wire.com, subscribe to our newsletter, or interact with our basketball coverage. By using our website, you agree to the practices described below.

Information We Collect

We collect information to provide a better experience for basketball fans and improve our coverage:

  • Personal Information You Provide: When you sign up for our newsletter, submit a contact form, or send us an email, we may collect your name, email address, and message content.
  • Usage Data: We gather non-identifiable data such as IP address, browser type, device type, pages visited, time spent on pages, and referring site.
  • Cookies & Tracking Technologies: Like most websites, we use cookies and analytics tools to enhance site performance, personalize content, and understand visitor behavior.

How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Deliver NBA news, game recaps, and analysis that match your interests.
  • Send newsletters and updates if you have opted in.
  • Monitor website performance and improve user experience.
  • Maintain site security and prevent misuse.
  • Comply with legal obligations where necessary.

Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share anonymized, aggregated data with trusted third-party providers (analytics, hosting, email services) who are contractually obligated to use the data only for the purposes of providing their services.

Cookies & Tracking

Cookies are small data files stored on your device. We use them to:

  • Remember your preferences (newsletter opt-in, region-specific content).
  • Deliver relevant basketball coverage and highlight trending articles.
  • Measure traffic, engagement, and content performance.

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain site features like saved preferences.

Third-Party Services

We use analytics providers such as Google Analytics to help us understand traffic trends, and may use affiliate tracking for basketball-related products, tickets, or services. Clicking on an affiliate link may result in a commission at no extra cost to you.

Email Communications

If you subscribe to our newsletter, we will use your email address to send NBA news, fantasy basketball advice, and major event updates. You can unsubscribe anytime via the link at the bottom of each email.

Data Retention & Security

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal requirements, or resolve disputes. Industry-standard encryption and access controls are used to protect your information, but no online transmission is 100% secure.

Children’s Privacy

The Sports Wire does not knowingly collect data from individuals under the age of 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child, we will delete it promptly.

International Users

If you are visiting from outside the United States, please note that your data may be processed in countries where data protection laws differ from your own. By using our site, you consent to this transfer.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request corrections or updates to inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data (“right to be forgotten”).
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time.

To exercise these rights, email us at contact@the-sports-wire.com

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. Updates will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
